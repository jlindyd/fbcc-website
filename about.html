Our Roots

Our History

Fort Bend Community Church was founded on June 1, 1997, when 281 members — a mix of English and Chinese-speaking Christians — were sent out by Houston Chinese Church to plant a new congregation in the Fort Bend area.

After meeting at various Sugar Land locations, the church relocated to its current Missouri City home in 1998. What began as a church plant has grown into a thriving community serving the greater Fort Bend area.

Today, FBCC ministers to approximately 1,500 weekly attendees through over 100 small groups and five worship services in Mandarin, Cantonese, English (adult), youth, and children's formats — across three campuses.

How We Live

Core Values

Worship

Expository preaching, music with spiritual power, creative communication, and unified service planning that honors God and builds up the body.

Evangelism

Cross-cultural outreach locally and globally. We equip all members and follow up within 36 hours of conversion. Evangelism is caught, not just taught.

Discipleship

Mentoring relationships, accountability, and prayer as a key pastoral ministry. We rely on the Holy Spirit to transform lives from the inside out.

Teamwork

Unified vision and effective strategy. We foster a positive, encouragement-based environment with clear roles and a shared direction in Christ.

Family Ministry

Strong marriages produce healthy families. The church partners with parents, emphasizes professional counseling, and casts vision for youth beyond academics.

Community Ministry

Fort Bend County is our primary focus. We meet community needs through prayer, service, and family support — loving our neighbors as ourselves.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

We affirm the fundamental doctrines of the Christian faith as revealed in Scripture.

The Bible

The 66 books of Scripture are divinely inspired, fully authoritative, and the ultimate standard for faith and life.

The Trinity

One God eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — equal in nature, distinct in person.

Jesus Christ

God's only begotten Son, virgin-born, fully human and fully divine, crucified for our sins and bodily resurrected.

The Holy Spirit

Our counselor and sanctifier who indwells believers, empowering us to live godly lives and fulfill God's mission.

Salvation

Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — a free gift to all who repent and believe.

Resurrection & Judgment

The future bodily resurrection of the dead and the final judgment of all people by Jesus Christ.
