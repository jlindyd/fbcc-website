Our Story

Serving the Fort Bend Community Since Day One

Fort Bend Community Church began with a vision to build a church that reflects the diversity and warmth of our community — a place where people from all backgrounds can encounter the living God.

Today we gather across three campuses in Missouri City, Pearland, and Richmond, worshipping in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. Our heart is to love God, love people, and serve our city.
